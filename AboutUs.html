Why are we doing this?

The basic idea of developing this site was to help people learn and improve their academic skills. Everyone can benefit from learning through our methodology since we provide a personalized learning environment. We focus on motivating people to learn by rewarding them with points based on certain criteria,So they can track their progress each step of the way.

Mission

We hope to empower learners to study and encourage others to join in and explore new ways to think and learn. We are students learning to develop websites, which will be helpful for other students and teachers in academic environments, even for anyone else intent on becoming more proficient and help them become more powerful with Knowledge.
